About me

I am a highly-skilled individual with strong experience working within time-critical environments. In my career, I have co-authored several research papers, as well as led and collaborated in several industry- and research-related projects. As well as research, I have undertaken significant teaching duties, including but not limited to: several guest lectures at various higher-education institutions in the UK, and developing masters-level courses in cybersecurity. At the core, my passion is learning and adapting new technologies to interesting problems and understanding population needs to develop proactive solutions which benefit people, society and the environment.

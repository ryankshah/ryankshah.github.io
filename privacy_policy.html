Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 13-08-2024

Introduction

ryankshah.com respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy explains how we manage cookies and comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), particularly in relation to Google AdSense and any potential Personal Identifiable Information (PII) that may be collected by third parties through our website.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Our website does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from visitors directly. We do not ask for or process any personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses. Any data that may be processed or collected is done solely by third-party services like Google AdSense, as described below.

2. Use of Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website does not use any cookies or tracking technologies to enhance your experience or track your behavior. The only cookies or tracking technologies present on our website are those used by Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service, which may collect information for the purpose of displaying personalized ads.

3. Google AdSense

Our website utilizes Google AdSense, an advertising service provided by Google Inc. ("Google"). Google AdSense may use cookies, web beacons, and other technologies to collect information about your visit to our website and other sites across the internet. This information may include:

  • The pages you visit on our site and other sites.
  • The advertisements you view and click on.
  • Information about your browser, device, and operating system.

Google may use this information to provide you with personalized advertisements based on your interests. Please note that Google, as a third-party service provider, may collect and process PII in accordance with its own privacy policies.

4. GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the following rights apply to you under the GDPR:

  • Right to Access: You have the right to request access to any personal data that Google may have collected about you through our website.
  • Right to Rectification: You have the right to request corrections to any inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: You have the right to request the deletion of personal data that has been collected, subject to certain conditions.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes.

To exercise these rights, please contact Google directly. We do not have access to or control over the data collected by Google.

5. CPRA Compliance

If you are a resident of California, the following rights apply to you under the CPRA:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to know what personal data is being collected, used, or shared.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain conditions.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal data to third parties.

To exercise these rights, please use the opt-out mechanisms provided by Google AdSense or contact Google directly.

6. Managing Cookies

Since our website does not use cookies for any purposes other than those related to Google AdSense, managing cookies will primarily involve managing third-party cookies set by Google. You can control and manage these cookies in several ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to view certain advertisements.
  • Opt-Out Tools: You can opt out of interest-based advertising by Google by visiting Google Ad Settings or by using industry opt-out tools such as the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) Opt-Out Tool or the Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA) Opt-Out Tool.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
